About Kanwal Martial Arts Academy

Empowering individuals through martial arts, self-defense and fitness training since our founding at Pakistan Sports Complex, Islamabad.

Who We Are

Building Champions Since Day One

Kanwal Martial Arts Academy is dedicated to empowering individuals through martial arts, self-defense and fitness training. Based at Pakistan Sports Complex, Liaqat Gymnasium on Srinagar Highway, Islamabad, we serve students of all ages — from young children to adults.

We believe martial arts is not only a sport but a way of life that teaches confidence, discipline and leadership. Under the guidance of Miss Sualeha Kanwal — Pakistan's first female martial arts coach to lead a girls' cadet college — our academy has grown into a premier training destination in the capital.

Our Direction

Mission & Vision

Our Mission

To provide quality martial arts education and develop confident, disciplined and responsible individuals who contribute positively to their families, communities and nation.

We achieve this through structured training, experienced coaching and a supportive community that nurtures every student's potential.

Our Vision

To become one of Pakistan's leading martial arts academies — recognized for excellence in training, student development and producing champions who excel in competitions and in life.

We aspire to make martial arts accessible to every child and adult across Islamabad and Pakistan.

What We Stand For

Our Core Values

These six values guide everything we do — from how we teach to how we treat every student and parent.

Discipline

Consistent practice and structured learning build the self-discipline that extends beyond the dojo into every area of life.

Respect

We foster mutual respect among students, instructors and families — the cornerstone of every traditional martial art.

Integrity

We act with honesty and fairness in all interactions, holding our students to the highest ethical standards.

Leadership

We develop natural leaders — individuals who inspire and guide others through their example and dedication.

Excellence

We strive for the highest standards in instruction, technique and student achievement across every program.

Confidence

Building genuine self-confidence is at the heart of our academy — we empower students to believe in themselves.
