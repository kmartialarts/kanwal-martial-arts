Meet Our Head Coach

Pakistan's pioneering female martial arts coach, dedicated to developing champions in Islamabad and beyond.

Head Coach

Miss Sualeha Kanwal

Miss Sualeha Kanwal is recognized as Pakistan's first female martial arts coach to lead a girls' cadet college — a historic achievement that reflects her dedication, skill and pioneering spirit in Pakistani sports.

She is committed to empowering youth through martial arts, self-defense and fitness training. Under her leadership, Kanwal Martial Arts Academy has grown into a premier institution at Pakistan Sports Complex, Islamabad, producing disciplined, confident and capable martial artists across all age groups.
